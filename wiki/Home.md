# Jarvis Wiki

Welcome to the official Jarvis wiki.

Jarvis is a local-first AI assistant product that runs on top of Ollama and provides:

- Desktop app
- Terminal CLI
- OpenAI-compatible local API
- Built-in Obsidian vault integration
- Local RAG over your notes/files
